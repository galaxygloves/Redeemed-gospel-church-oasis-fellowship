Our Ministries

Men's Ministry

Empowering men to grow in faith, leadership, and service. The Men's Ministry provides fellowship, mentorship, and outreach opportunities for men of all ages.

Children's Ministry

Nurturing the next generation in the love and knowledge of Christ. Our Children's Ministry offers engaging Bible lessons, fun activities, and a safe environment for kids.

Youth Ministry

Inspiring and equipping young people to live boldly for Christ. The Youth Ministry focuses on discipleship, worship, and community impact for teens and young adults.

Mercy Ministry

Demonstrating Christ's love through acts of compassion and service. The Mercy Ministry organizes outreach programs, support groups, and community assistance initiatives.
