Reverend Harrison Maingi

Founder & Senior Pastor

Rev. Harrison Maingi is the visionary Founder and Senior Pastor of Redeemed Gospel Church Oasis Fellowship.

With a heart for transformation, he has dedicated his life to raising disciples, empowering believers, and impacting communities with the love and hope of Christ. His leadership is marked by compassion, integrity, and a relentless pursuit of God's purpose for every individual.

Reverend Maingi is known for his inspiring sermons, mentorship, and commitment to outreach, making Oasis Fellowship a beacon of faith and service in Nakuru and beyond.
